Research purposes

This research page compiles websites that inspire our footwear brand's design direction, user experience, and overall aesthetics. Each website has been carefully selected for its unique approach to footwear design, showcasing innovative features, and providing a seamless user experience. Our goal is to draw inspiration from these sources to create a footwear website that not only captivates our audience but also reflects our brand's identity and values.

Design Elements to Consider

Based on our research of these websites, we should consider incorporating these key elements.

  • High-quality product photography from multiple angles
  • Remote delivery logistic
  • Advanced filtering options
  • Product customization tools
  • Sustainability storytelling
  • Wide payment options
  • Minimalist design

By implementing these features, Paila was able to create a successful user-centric e-commerce platform tailored to the specific needs and interests of footwear buyers, enhancing both engagement and satisfaction.
